Right to Choose (RTC)

The practical route by which eligible participants may access faster ADHD assessment pathways, where clinically appropriate and governed.

QbTest

An objective assessment support tool that may sit within an RTC-enabled pathway.

Continuity around referral and waiting

OTOS remains non-clinical: it provides continuity and receipted follow-through so the handoff does not vanish.

Guardrails

  • No diagnosis, prescribing or clinical decision-making by OTOS.
  • No EPR write-back.
  • No commitment to a full rollout by giving feedback.
