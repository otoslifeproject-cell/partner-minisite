What we need from this group

Support the thread after signposting.
Without adding record burden.

For community, employment and therapy partners, the ask is lighter than for clinical services. You provide the next step, the safe destination, and the human presence. OTOS holds the thread around it.

A safe next step

Offer a real, practical destination that can appear in OTOS prompts — a session, a course, a conversation. Something real that someone can actually do while they wait for clinical next steps.

Non-clinical drift signals

Help identify whether someone is engaging, going quiet, or needs a safe re-engagement prompt — based on what you see in your own interaction, not on access to their clinical record.

Clear boundaries, no surprises

Support joined-up recovery without requiring full clinical record access. OTOS is designed so you can participate without becoming a clinical stakeholder in the pathway.

Clear boundaries

What OTOS will never do.
And what stays entirely with you.

Guardrails — always

  • No new clinical record to maintain.
  • No diagnosis, prescribing or clinical decision-making by OTOS.
  • No EPR write-back or NHS record access required.
  • No commitment to a full rollout by joining the scoping conversation.

Questions to resolve together

  • Which services or sessions work best as continuity next steps?
  • What wording feels right for messages sent in your name?
  • What interaction signals are appropriate to log?
  • Who needs to be in the scoping conversation from your team?
