The 12-week Cambridge pre-pilot.
What it asks of you.

OTOS Continuity™ is non-clinical continuity infrastructure with governed message-delivery and micro-check-in capability. It sits around existing services so the person’s journey can be held between handoffs, waiting lists, discharge points, recovery support and crisis risk.

What we askPartner routes
The shared ask

Minimal ask.
Maximum visibility.

This is a request to test a bounded continuity model, not a request to change clinical responsibility or buy a new clinical system.

Named contact

One named contact per participating organisation to answer quick workflow questions, confirm local red lines and help shape the safest route.

Human reviewer

Drift or escalation items are reviewed by a human. The reviewer records the decision and closes the loop. If the burden is too much, we change the model.

Route clarity

Help define what OTOS must not touch, especially around clinical responsibility, records, consent, escalation and safeguarding.
