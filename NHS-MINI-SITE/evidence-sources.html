Policy alignment

Not ahead of the evidence.
Precisely within it.

  • NHS 10 Year Health Plan — hospital to community, analogue to digital, sickness to prevention.
  • NHS ADHD Taskforce — undiagnosed ADHD framed as a major and under-addressed public health issue.
  • 250 NHS Community Hubs — the continuity layer is designed to operate naturally in community health infrastructure.
  • DHSC addiction healthcare goals — government investment in addiction technology that addresses structural fragmentation.
“This is not a request to fund a full rollout. It is a request to fund a clear question: does a receipted continuity thread, applied to this cohort, measurably reduce silent disengagement and increase the likelihood that people reach the next meaningful step?”
