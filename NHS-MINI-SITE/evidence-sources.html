LabelMeaningHow it is used
Published sourceA figure from published research, government source, NHS source or official dataset.Used as evidence or comparator, with source retained in the evidence register.
Local sourceA local service figure or partner-confirmed number.Used only where the local source is recorded and can be validated.
OTOS modelling estimateA calculated planning figure based on published denominators and assumptions.Used to size the opportunity, not as an observed fact.
AssumptionA scenario value used to test sensitivity.Must remain visible until replaced by local evidence.
Key supporting evidence

ADHD in addiction cohorts

Research supports a significant undiagnosed ADHD cohort within substance-use populations. OTOS uses this as the clinical rationale for identifying the cohort early.

Continuity and follow-through

The pre-pilot tests whether visible, receipted continuity can reduce silent disengagement and support people reaching the next meaningful step.

Local service pressure

Published and locally supplied service-pressure figures are kept distinct from OTOS modelling estimates.

Policy alignment

The model aligns with hospital-to-community, neighbourhood health, no-wrong-door and prevention-first direction of travel.
