Claims we are not making

What the evidence does not say

OTOS is not claiming that ADHD treatment automatically resolves addiction, that OTOS itself produces START-level outcomes, or that the pre-pilot will save money without local evaluation. The pre-pilot tests a narrower operational question: can a receipted continuity layer reduce silent disengagement in a defined, already-visible cohort?
