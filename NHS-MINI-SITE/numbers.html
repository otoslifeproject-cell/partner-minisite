Adult addiction treatment data are stronger than adult ADHD service data. Addiction data are often Local Authority based, while ADHD service data are NHS provider or national-proxy based.

LabelMeaningHow it is used
Published source A figure from published research, government, NHS or official dataset. Used as evidence or comparator, with source retained in the evidence register.
Local source A local service figure or partner-confirmed number. Used only where the local source is recorded and can be validated.
OTOS modelling estimate A calculated planning figure based on published denominators and assumptions. Used to size the opportunity, not as an observed fact.
Assumption A scenario value used to test sensitivity. Remains visible until replaced by local evidence from the pre-pilot.

What would weaken the model

The 800–900 local cohort is a planning estimate, not a counted register. The model should be weakened or revised if local validation shows materially lower ADHD/addiction overlap, fewer repeat crisis presentations, poor engagement with check-ins, or no measurable difference in handoff receipt / silent disengagement over the 12-week period.
