Where this route fits

A credible holding layer.
Not passive waiting.

A referral is a signpost. A hand-off receipt is a connection.

Between appointments

RCE Wellbeing Hub belongs in the pilot framing as a real, existing between-appointments support option while people are waiting or moving between services.

Not a clinical decision-maker

In OTOS terms, RCE is not a clinical decision-maker and not a pathway owner. It is one of the best existing places the continuity thread can point to.

Co-written prompts, real next steps

Co-written, non-clinical prompts can point to a real course, session or next step. A reply or interaction becomes a logged continuity signal on the token journey.

What this is not asking

Keep the boundaries clear.
Keep the burden low.

Guardrails

  • No new clinical record to maintain.
  • No diagnosis, prescribing or clinical decision-making by OTOS.
  • No EPR write-back.
  • No commitment to a full rollout by giving feedback.

Questions to resolve

  • Who needs to be involved?
  • What touchpoints are realistic?
  • What time and effort is acceptable?
  • What support wording would be comfortable?
