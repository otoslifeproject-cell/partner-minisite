What we need from CGL & CRS

Cohort identification.
Visible follow-through.

You see people repeatedly. You know who keeps coming back. OTOS helps you hold the thread between those visits — so the next step is receipted, not assumed.

Identify the right cohort

Help shape the hand-picked cohort with CPFT and other partners: people still in trouble, repeatedly touching services, where ADHD may be the underlying driver of their addiction.

Define the touchpoints

Tell us what can be logged in seconds, what should be receipted, and where drift should surface for human review. Your keyworker team stays in control of every decision.

Keep clinical control yours

OTOS surfaces the signals. Your team makes every decision. No new clinical record. No EPR write-back. No diagnosis or prescribing — ever.

The ask now is deliberately small because the future system is designed to reduce your burden, not add to it.

Clear boundaries

What OTOS will never do.

Guardrails — always

  • No new clinical record to maintain.
  • No diagnosis, prescribing or clinical decision-making by OTOS.
  • No EPR write-back.
  • No commitment to a full rollout by giving feedback or joining the scoping call.
