You see people repeatedly. You know who keeps coming back. OTOS helps you hold the thread between those visits — so the next step is receipted, not assumed.

A Tuesday afternoon scenario

Your 2pm doesn't show. Currently: noted as DNA, case continues. With OTOS: no-show logged as drift signal after threshold exceeded. Keyworker reviews. Decides whether to re-engage. Closes the loop. One click. One decision. Recorded.

Identify the right cohort

Help shape the hand-picked 50-person cohort: people still in trouble, repeatedly touching services, where ADHD may be the underlying driver of their addiction. You already know who they are.

Clinical control stays yours

OTOS surfaces the signals. Your team makes every decision. No new clinical record. No EPR write-back. No diagnosis or prescribing — ever.

What OTOS never does

  • No new clinical record to maintain
  • No diagnosis, prescribing or clinical decision-making
  • No EPR write-back
  • No commitment to full rollout by joining the scoping call

Questions to resolve together

  • Who needs to be in the scoping conversation?
  • What touchpoints are realistic for your keyworker team?
  • What time and effort is acceptable week-to-week?
  • What support wording works within your governance?
